Shakshuka

Description

An easy breakfast/lunch, that takes under 10 minutes to make!

Serves: 1

Ingredients

  • A jar of crushed tomatoes
  • Spinach, leaves torn or shredded
  • A couple of mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 eggs
  • Paprika
  • Ground Cumin
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Using a medium sized wok on, pour a splash of oil and heat on medium settings
  2. Once up to temperature, add the mushrooms and close the lid for a few minutes
  3. Once the mushrooms are almost cooked, remove the lid, and add the tomatoes and all the spices. Mix gently.
  4. Add the spinach to the mix and stir in.
  5. Using your mixing spoon, create a gap in the mixture to crack your egg into. Do this process twice, making sure the eggs have enough spacing.
  6. Close the lid, and cook for just under 4 minutes. Turn the heat down a little if it's bubbling too vigorously
  7. Open the lid, check the eggs are cooked and serve!
