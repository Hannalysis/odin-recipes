Simple Chicken & Rice

Description

An easy mid-week evening meal, that takes ~ 20 minutes to make!

Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 160g White Rice
  • 350g Chicken Thighs or Breast
  • Green Pepper
  • Baby Button Mushrooms
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Ground Coriander
  • Cream of Chicken and Mushroom Soup

Steps

  1. Boil a medium pot half filled with water, and add a pinch of salt
  2. As the water comes to the boil, set a wok to a medium meat with a splash of oil of your choosing
  3. Add the White Rice to the pot for 10 minutes...
  4. ...Whilst the rice is cooking, add the chicken to the wok with a teaspoon of coriander
  5. Stir the wok, and add the garlic until the chicken is well whitened
  6. Turn down the heat by an increment or two on the wok, and add the diced peppers
  7. Stir and cook for a couple of minutes, and add a handful of baby button mushrooms
  8. Add the soup, and stir frequently, until the rice is cooked
  9. Drain the rice, and place the contents on the plates
  10. Serve the chicken and veg, and you're ready to eat!
